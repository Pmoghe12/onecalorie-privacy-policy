Delete Account — OneCalorie

Last updated: April 15, 2026

You can request deletion of your OneCalorie account and associated data at any time.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to:
    support.onecalorie@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject: Delete my OneCalorie account
  3. In the email body, include:
    • The email address used to sign in to OneCalorie
    • Your name (optional)
    • Your phone number (optional, if you added one)

What data will be deleted

  • Your OneCalorie profile and settings
  • Foods you created
  • Meal collections you created
  • Food logs (calories/macros)
  • Water logs

Retention and timeline

We typically process deletion requests within 7 days. We may retain minimal information if required to comply with legal obligations, prevent fraud/abuse, or resolve disputes.

Note: If you no longer have access to your account email, please contact us from any email address and include as much information as possible so we can verify ownership.
